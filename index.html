Serenity
Coffee Shop

Curtis and Jennie live right here in Okeechobee and have been here for over 40 years. Another way a cup makes a difference is why they opened. They had a calling to open Serenity in 2011 in an effort to offer a place where people could receive counseling; a place that would be free and neutral for all parties involved. Surprisingly, Serenity would turn into more than just a place to offer counseling. They have a vision: selling coffee that supportsx missions. Serenity is a place where laughter happens and friends are made; a place to "get off the grind" of this world and find some Serenity.
